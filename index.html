The 4Ds Metrics model is an approach designed for the rapid and efficient development of desirable and feasible products or services, as well as viable business models. This is achieved by applying a combination of the iterative 4Ds namely: Define, Design, Develop, and Deploy; as well as bringing into play design thinking. In simple words, 4Ds Metrics represent a process of developing a new business idea to a fully functional business. The 4Ds Metrics approach was founded and designed by TUYIHIMBAZE Serge, Founder and CEO of 4Ds Metrics Ltd. and Co-founder and CEO of LEAPR LABS Ltd. Kigali-Rwanda.

We help innovators, startups and companies to turn their ideas into desirable, feasible and viable products by using the 4Ds Metrics, an automated tool that diagnose a product to identify gaps and provide guidance to filling the gaps. The 4Ds metrics are define, design, develop and Deploy respectively. Below are the objects of each.

Define
