The 4Ds Metrics model is an approach designed for the rapid and efficient development of desirable and feasible products or services, as well as viable business models. This is achieved by applying a combination of the iterative 4Ds namely: Define, Design, Develop, and Deploy; as well as bringing into play design thinking. In simple words, 4Ds Metrics represent a process of developing a new business idea to a fully functional business. The 4Ds Metrics approach was founded and designed by TUYIHIMBAZE Serge, Founder and CEO of 4Ds Metrics Ltd. and Co-founder and CEO of LEAPR LABS Ltd. Kigali-Rwanda.

Our Services

We help innovators, startups and companies to turn their ideas into desirable, feasible and viable products by using the 4Ds Metrics, an automated tool that diagnoses a product to identify gaps and provide guidance to filling the gaps. The 4Ds Metrics are Define, Design, Develop and Deploy respectively. Below are the objectives of each.

Define

To understand the customer's needs, so as to find possible solutions; while considering the entire solution space.

Learn more

Design

a detailed design of the proposed solution and shape the product’s or service’s desirability and feasibility.

Learn more

Develop

the product or service that serves the identified needs ensuring viability and sustainability of the business, as well as it’s impact on the environment.

Learn more

Deploy

the minimum viable product (MVP) for customer validation, traction building and go-to market strategy testing.
